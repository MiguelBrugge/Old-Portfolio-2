Team Roles

I have done the Belbin and Idrlabs teamroles test and here are my results. I also do think the results are accurate.


Belbin

Specialist Brings in-depth knowledge of a key area to the team. Single-minded, self-starting and dedicated. Provide knowledge and skills.

Monitor Provides a logical eye, making impartial judgements where required and weighs up the team's options in a dispassionate way. Sober, strategic and discerning. Sees all options and judges accurately.


Idrlabs

Diplomat Sympathetic empaths who keep the team running smoothly. They are good listeners and mediators, talented at smoothing over conflicts and helping opposed parties understand one another without becoming confrontational.

Judge-Appraiser Fair and logically minded observers and evaluators of what is going on with the team. Since they are good at detaching themselves from bias, they are often the ones to see all available options with the greatest clarity and impartiality.
